Data Visualizations

Chart 1: Risk vs. Return by Fund

Takeaway: Funds show a wide range of risk-return tradeoffs, with some strategies delivering higher returns without taking proportionally more risk. Using filtering, Strategies like Relative Value and Multi-Strategy tend to cluster toward more efficient outcomes. Credit/Fixed income is notable, offering lower volatility and returns, with much less variability than other fund types.

Chart 2: Sharpe Ratio by Strategy

Takeaway: Sharpe Ratios vary meaningfully across strategies, indicating large differences in risk-adjusted performance. A few strategies consistently outperform, while others show much lower and more volatile results.

Chart 3: Average Monthly Return by Strategy

Takeaway: Monthly returns exhibit clear clusters where multiple strategies perform well or poorly at the same time, suggesting exposure to shared market conditions. Some strategies show more stable month-to-month behavior than others.

Chart 4: Fund Size vs. Sharpe Ratio

Takeaway: There is only a weak relationship between fund size and Sharpe Ratio: Larger funds do not reliably deliver stronger performance. This suggests that scale alone does not determine risk-adjusted results.

Chart 5: Distribution of Annualized Returns

Takeaway: The distribution of returns is skewed, with many funds clustered near modest positive performance and a tail of both outperformers and underperformers. The gap between mean and median highlights the influence of a few high-return funds.

Chart 6: Strategy Performance Scorecard

Takeaway: No single strategy leads across all performance metrics; strengths in one dimension often come with weaknesses in another. This reinforces the importance of evaluating strategies across multiple measures rather than relying on a single indicator.

Chart 7 (D3): Strategy-Level Summary

Takeaway: Switching between metrics reveals that strategy rankings shift depending on whether returns, risk, or risk-adjusted performance is prioritized. The dropdown interaction highlights these tradeoffs and helps compare strategies more directly.
