Welcome to the Hedge Fund Performance Analysis Project!

Within this project, our team aims to provide an indepth analysis of hedge fund performance data. We aim to accomplisht this by examining the realtionships between risk, return, assets under management (AUM), investmnet startegies, and fund characteristics.

Through interactive visualizations and explanations, this website will walk through a step-by-step analysis that our team used. From data cleaning to final conclusion, we will answer key questions about hedge fund performance to provide potential investors with a risk-return profile that matches their needs.
